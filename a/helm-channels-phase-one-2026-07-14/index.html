03 / boundary

What phase one deliberately does not claim

No collaborative editor or remote identity. This remains a single-owner local archive.

No automatic cloud sync, multi-writer merge, access-control list, or recall of immutable URLs already distributed. Those need a separate trust model.

Sources and method
